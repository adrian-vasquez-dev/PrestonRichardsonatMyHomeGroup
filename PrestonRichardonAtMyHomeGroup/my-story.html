Hello, I am Preston! I am a Realtor® in the Valley of the Sun. Better known as Phoenix, Arizona. I can help you buy, sell, or rent your next home anywhere in the metro phoenix area. You can't find a roadmap or step by step guide to becoming a Realtor®. I originally thought I wanted to be a teacher. After receiving my bachelor’s degree I continued on to earn my masters in college administration. Along the way I met my future wife.

My fiancé and I worked our behinds off to purchase our first home in 2018. We decided to purchase a fixer upper because of the thriving Phoenix market. At first the project seemed overwhelming, but we soon learned how blessed we were to be able to personalize our home. During the renovation I fell in love with the process of fixing a home. I loved seeing my fiancé’s face light up every time I finished a bedroom, installed a floor, or upgraded her “spa like” bathroom. I wanted to provide the same experience to others looking for a new home. It was at that moment I decided to become a Realtor®.Let me help you find your next home. It would be my pleasure to join you on your journey to your first home.
