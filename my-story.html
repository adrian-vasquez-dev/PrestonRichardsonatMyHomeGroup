Hello, I am Preston! I am a realtor in the Valley of the Sun in Phoenix, Arizona. I can help you buy, sell, or rent your next home anywhere in the valley. Becoming a realtor doesn't have a roadmap to it's profession. I pursued my bachelor's degree to become a teacher and continued on to receive my masters in college administration. I have always enjoyed helping people be the best they can be.

My fiancé and I worked our behinds off and were able to purchase out first home in 2018. We had to purchase a fixer upper because of the thriving market in Phoenix. At first it seemed overwhelming, but we had no idea how much of a blessing it was going to be to be able to personalize our home. During the renovation, I fell in love with the process of fixing a home and seeing my fiancé's face light up every time I finished a room, the floor, or her "spa like" bathroom. I wanted to see that look on the face of all the people I could help in Phoenix finding their home. I decided to become a realtor. So, let me help you find your next home. Give me the pleasure of seeing you be happy your first day turning the key to your first home.
